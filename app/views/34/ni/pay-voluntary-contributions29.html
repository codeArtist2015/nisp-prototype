Payment by cheque

You can send your payment to HMRC directly. Make your cheque payable to ‘HM Revenue and Customs only’ followed by your payment reference.

Include a note with:

  • your name, address and telephone number
  • your National Insurance number
  • the years you’re paying for
  • whether you want a receipt

Post it to:

HM Revenue and Customs
National Insurance Contributions and Employer Office
Benton Park View
Newcastle upon Tyne
NE98 1ZZ

Allow 3 working days for the payment to reach HMRC.

It can take around 28 days to update your National Insurance record with any voluntary contributions you make.
