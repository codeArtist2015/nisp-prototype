Get a State Pension statement

You can get a State Pension forecast based on your National Insurance record, and find out how to improve it, if you're:

  • a man born after 5 April 1951
  • a woman born after 5 April 1953

Check your State Pension online
This new service is currently in ‘beta’ - it’s being tested and improved.

Other ways to get a statement

If you were born before these dates or have worked and paid National Insurance in the Isle of Man contact the Future Pension Centre.

If you’re 50 or over your statement will be calculated according to new State Pension rules.
