Your State Pension

You can get your State Pension on 1 December 2057 when you’ll be 67, your forecast is

£155.65 a week

£676.80 a month, £8,121.59 a year

Your forecast

  • is not a guarantee and is based on the current law
  • does not include any increase due to inflation

You need to continue to contribute National Insurance to reach your forecast

You have 3 years on your record. You need another:

  • 7 years on your record before you can get any State Pension
  • 32 years to get the most you can

If you have lived or worked overseas (opens in new tab), you may be able to use your time abroad to make your years in the UK qualify for the purposes of getting any State Pension.

£155.65 is the most you can get

When you reach £155.65, you still need to pay National Insurance until 2057 as it funds other state benefits and the NHS.
